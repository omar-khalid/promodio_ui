Participate in promoting a song

Traditionally the only way a song gets promoted is through mainstream media. Promod.io enables everyone to participate in making a song popular.

Power to Independent Musicians

Although there are more independent musicians than ever before, with an exception of a few, popular music are still being driven by big budgets of recording labels. Promod.io enables small indie musicians to promote their songs to a wide audience, even, without a marketing budget.

The Community

Promodions are passionate about music, and can often be heard having long and deep discussions about song feel, hook, structure, dynamics etc. If you are one of us, this is your home.

All about the music

A place where you can play fresh music all day, all night, download sounds you like, and promote music that invigorates you, and upload your own music to share with the world.

WHY PROMODO.IO?

Supporting independent musicians everywhere

Great music are created everyday by musicians everywhere in the world. But only a very small fraction get into mainstream media. We hope to level the playing field for every independent musician out there, so that every music will be popular based on its own merits, and not based on an opinion of a small group of music executives. Give us a hand in making this dream possible.
